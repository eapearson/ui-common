The "nice" series of date & time formatters

This series of methods specializes in readable, compact, yet precise representations of dates and times. They are minimally if at all configurable - the code is the place to make format changes.

For example, 2015-06-21 would be represented as Jun 21, 2015, the time 13:00 as 1pm, and the date range 2015-3-3 to 2015-3-7 as Mar 3-7, 2015.

niceDate

Mostly for internal usage, it takes a Date object and produces a compact date string that makes sense "today" -- that is, in the context of today's date.

niceTime

Mostly for internal usage, it takes a Date object and produces a compact time string.
